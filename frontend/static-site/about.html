About Found-It

Found-It is a campus lost & found platform built for Mohan Babu University students and staff. Our mission is to reunite people with lost belongings across campus quickly and safely.

How it works

  1. Report an item you found or lost using the Upload/Report pages.
  2. Include clear photos and contact information.
  3. Claim items by contacting the poster through the secure messaging flow.
